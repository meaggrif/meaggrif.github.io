Are there scholarships available for graduate students?

Graduate students can receive financial aid by applying for a grant or a graduate assistantship. However, there are no scholarships sepcific to graduate students.

If I do not receive enough financial aid, can I get a loan?

Your financial aid package will include the maximum amount of federal loans that you are eligible for. If you need additional funding once you receive your financial aid package, try applying for a private loan!

Are there any jobs on campus that provide tuition remission?

Yes! As a graduate student, you will receive tuition remission if you are awarded an assistantship. As an undergraduate, none of the jobs include full tuition remission, but if you are hired as an RA, you do not have to pay for housing.
