Federal Aid

Each student has the opportunity to apply for federal aid. Be sure to complete the FAFSA application as soon as possible when it is made available each year. The aid is often in the form of loans and in some cases, grants. You will be notified of the federal aid you have been awarded upon acceptance.

General Scholarships

There are a few scholarships that applicants may apply for separately once accepted to College. They vary by major and program. Some include scholarships for music majors based on vocal or instrumental talent, or awards for STEM students that show innovative promise in campus events such as hackathons. Contact each major department for more information.

Other Scholarships

Some scholarship at College are awarded upon admission without a separate application. During application review, we will see if an applicant is eligible for one of two special scholarships - one for academic merit or one for athletic acheivements. If eligible, there will be further review to determine who is awarded a scholarship and you will be notified of the award in your acceptance letter.
