Recreation Center

Students can participate in a healthy lifestyle by getting active at the College recreation center. Whether you like to run, swim, or go to group classes, the recreation center is a great place to be!

Intramural & Club Sports

Students that are not on official College sports teams can still participate in team sports on a club or intramural team. Club sports teams require students to try out and attend weekly practices. Intramural sports are more laid back. Students can put together teams with their friends and hold unofficial practices for weekly games!

Group Classes

Are you interested in fitness but not sure where to start? Consider coming to a group class at the recreation center! Group classes are guided classes in various areas of fitness. From cycling to body pump, there is surely a class for you.
