Housing On Campus & Off Campus

  1. Home
  2. Housing

Transfer Dorms

There is some space for transfer students in the transfer dorm on campus. Only transfer students that are freshmen or sophomores will be eligible for on campus housing. If you submit the separate on campus housing application with your College application, you will be notified upon acceptance if you have been given a spot in the transfer dorm. Otherwise, transfer students will have to seek off campus housing options.

Off Campus Housing

There are a lot of housing options surrounding the College campus. Students can find housing in apartment building or houses shared with other College students! Plus, most of the off campus housing is location on a College bus route.

Dorms for Freshmen & Sophomores

Undergraduate Freshmen and Sophomores are guaranteed a dorm room upon acceptance. There are eight undergraduate dorms on campus. Your acceptance letter will contain information about your assignment.
