Can I bring guests to the dining halls and recreation center?

Each undergradute meal plan inlcudes 2 guest passes. Students may use these passes to bring guests into the dining halls. If a student is out of passes, guests can still enter with a student by paying a fee of $15. Students can also bring one guest at a time into the recreation center for $10. The $10 guest pass is valid for the whole day it is purchased.

Can students outside of the music major participate in College theatre productions?

Unfortunately, only music students may participate in the College theatre productions because participation is required for thr major. However, students can be involved in music through student clubs, such as the campus record label.

Do the residence halls have any ammenities?

Residence halls have varying ammentities based on the building. However, all buildings have laundry rooms, group study areas, and vending machines.
