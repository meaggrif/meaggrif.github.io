  2. Financial Aid

Academic Scholarships

Students at University have the opportunity to receive multiple Academic scholarships of up to $10,000 annually. To be eligible, you must have a minimum 3.5 unweighted GPA. No application is neccessary. You will be notified upon acceptance if you have been chosen to receive an Academic scholarship.

Athletic Scholarships

Students at University have the opportunity to receive multiple Athletic scholarships of up to $10,000 annually. To be eligible, you must be recruited at one of our recruiting events, or by a recruiter at your high school. No application is neccessary. You will be notified upon acceptance if you have been chosen to be on a team at University and receive an athletics scholarship.

FAFSA

Students at University have the opportunity
to receive FAFSA funding. FAFSA is federal
student aid. The application is separate
on the FAFSA website and they will
determine your eligibility for federal
financial aid. You will be notified of your
financial aid award status and package at the
time of your acceptance.
