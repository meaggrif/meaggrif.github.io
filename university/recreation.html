Campus Recreation

Recreation Center

The University Recreation Center is a great place for students to get active! From the track to the pool to the weight room, every one can find something to do there.

Group Adventures

Do you love hiking and outdoor adventures? Join fellow students on a group adventure trip! The University Group Adventures include hiking and backpacking trips, weekend camping, and even bouldering.

Intramural & Club Sports

Intramural and club sports are a great way to remain engaged in team sports while you are a student. Intramural sports are great for athletes of all skill levels, and can even be teams of all of your friends! If you want a bit more competition and a more rigorous team, try out for a club sports team!
