Housing On Campus & Off Campus

Freshman & Sophomore Dorms

There are ten dorm buildings on campus for freshman and sophomore students. You will be assigned a room in one of the dorms and notified of your assignment upon acceptance.

Transfer Student Dorm

Transfer students have limited eligibility for on campus housing. If you are a freshman or sophomore Transfer student, you can apply to be assigned to a room in the Transfer dorm. However, transfer students that are juniors, seniors, or not assigned to the Transfer dorm will have to seek off campus housing.

Off Campus Housing

There are many options for off campus housing around University. Students that do not live on campus can find housing in apartments or shared houses, some of which are within walking distance to the campus or near a University bus stop!
