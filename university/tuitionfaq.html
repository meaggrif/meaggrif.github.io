How do I apply for an academic scholarship?

Academic awards are not determined by a separate scholarhsip application. Students with a minumum 3.7 unweighted GPA will be considered for an academic scholarship when they apply to University. You will be notified upon acceptance if you are being awarded an academic scholarship.

How do I apply for an athletic scholarship?

Athletic awards are not determined by a separate scholarhsip application. Students will be recruited at one of our recruiting events or at thier high school sports game by a University recruiter. You will be notified upon acceptance to both the university and a sports team at University if you have been chosen to receive an athletic scholarship.

Are there any loan options other than federal loans?

Yes! Many students finance their college education using private student loans. Private lenders are separate from University and FAFSA, so you will need to visit their websites or call their offices for details as they may vary.
