Student FAQs

Are transfer students eligible for on campus housing?

Transfer students that are at the freshman or sophomore credit level are eligible to apply for housing in our Transfer Dorm! If you are a transfer student at the junio or senior credit level, you will have to seek off campus housing.

Are there any sports teams for students that are not on official University teams?

Yes, there are sports teams for students that are not on the official University teams. Students can play on Intramural sports teams, or try out for the more competitive Club sports teams.

What ammenities does the University recreation center have?

The University recreation center has many ammenities! There is a track, a pool, and a full weight room. There are also studios for yoga and various types of training, as well as locker rooms with showers.
